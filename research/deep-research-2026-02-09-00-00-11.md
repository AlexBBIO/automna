Low-Cost Entry Tiers ($10\u201330/mo) vs. Free Trials\n\nThe debate between offering a free trial versus a low-cost paid entry tier is particularly relevant for AI companies facing high variable costs (GPU usage, API tokens).\n\n### 2.1 The Case for Paid Entry Tiers\nA \"paid trial\" or low-cost monthly tier acts as a **soft paywall**.
